# Invoice Management — BuilderDash

BuilderDash invoicing is connected directly to your jobs. No separate system. No re-entry. Raise, send, and track invoices from the same place you manage the work.

---

## Overview

Invoicing in BuilderDash covers:

- Generating invoices directly from jobs
- Multiple invoice types (full, deposit, milestone, variation)
- Professional branded PDF output
- Status tracking (sent, overdue, paid)
- VAT calculation and display

---

## Invoice Types

### Full Invoice
The total value of the job, raised at completion. Standard for smaller jobs.

### Deposit Invoice
Raised before work starts. Common for jobs where materials need to be purchased upfront, or to secure commitment from the client on a larger project.

Typical deposit structures:
- 25-30% upfront on larger fit-outs
- 50% on smaller jobs where material cost is significant

### Milestone / Stage Invoices
Raise invoices at agreed points during the job rather than waiting until completion. Common on longer projects.

Example milestones:
- Mobilisation
- First fix complete
- Second fix complete
- Practical completion
- Defects liability / retention release

### Variation Invoices
For work carried out outside the original quote scope. Keep variation invoices separate from the main job invoice so the billing is transparent.

## Generating an Invoice

Open the job → click **Raise Invoice** → select the type.

BuilderDash pre-populates:
- Your business name, address, and logo
- Client name and billing address
- Job description
- Line items (from the original quote, editable)
- Your standard payment terms

Review, adjust if needed, and send.

---

## Invoice Numbering

Invoices are numbered sequentially and automatically. Your invoice numbers are consistent and professional — no gaps, no duplicates.

---

## VAT

Set the VAT rate per line item. BuilderDash calculates and displays:
- Net amount per line
- VAT amount per line
- Total net
- Total VAT
- Gross total

Your VAT registration number appears on every invoice if added to your business profile.

---

## Sending Invoices

**From BuilderDash:** Send directly to the client's email. They receive a professional PDF. The invoice is logged as sent automatically.

**Download as PDF:** Download and send from your own email account if preferred.

---

## Payment Tracking

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Draft | Prepared but not yet sent |
| Sent | Sent to client, awaiting payment |
| Overdue | Past due date, unpaid |
| Paid | Payment received and logged |

Mark invoices as paid when you receive payment. BuilderDash updates the job status accordingly.

---

## Overdue Invoice Visibility

Your dashboard flags overdue invoices. At a glance you can see:
- Which invoices are outstanding
- How long they've been overdue
- Total amount outstanding across all jobs

This means you don't miss chasing money that's owed to you.

---

## Invoice History

Every invoice ever raised is stored against the relevant job. Useful for:
- Accounting and bookkeeping
- Responding to client queries
- VAT return preparation
- Proof of billing in disputes
