# Quote Management — BuilderDash

Professional quoting is the foundation of a well-run construction business. BuilderDash makes it fast to produce accurate, professional quotes and track them through to acceptance.

---

## Overview

The BuilderDash quoting system covers the full quote lifecycle:

- Create a quote from scratch or from a template
- Send to the client as a professional PDF
- Track status (draft, sent, accepted, declined, expired)
- Convert accepted quotes to jobs in one click

---

## Creating Quotes

Quotes in BuilderDash are structured documents, not free-form text. This keeps your quotes consistent and ensures nothing gets missed.

**Quote structure:**
- Client details (pulled from your client records)
- Job title and description
- Line items (description, quantity, unit price, VAT)
- Subtotal, VAT, and total
- Payment terms
- Validity date
- Notes and exclusions
- Your business branding

---

## Line Items

The line item section is where the detail lives. Break down your quote into individual items:

- Labour (by trade, by day rate, by task)
- Materials (itemised or grouped)
- Plant and equipment hire
- Subcontractor costs
- Preliminaries

The more clearly you break it down, the easier it is for the client to understand what they're paying for — and the fewer questions you'll get.

---

## Quote Validity

Set an expiry date on every quote. Material prices change. Your capacity changes. An open-ended quote can come back to haunt you months later.

BuilderDash flags expired quotes so you know which ones need refreshing before the client comes back.

---

## Sending Quotes

**Via BuilderDash:** Send the quote directly from the system. The client receives a professional email with the quote attached as a PDF.

**Download as PDF:** Download and send via your own email — gives you more control over the accompanying message.

---

## Quote Status Tracking

| Status | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Draft | Being worked on, not sent |
| Sent | Awaiting client decision |
| Accepted | Client approved — ready to start |
| Declined | Client turned it down |
| Expired | Validity date passed |

Track all your outstanding quotes in one view. Know which ones need chasing and which jobs are coming your way.

---

## Converting to a Job

When a quote is accepted, click **Convert to Job**. BuilderDash creates the job record with all the quote details pre-populated. No re-entry.

The quote and job remain linked — so you can always trace back to what was originally agreed.

---

## Variations

When the scope of work changes after a quote is accepted, raise a variation. This keeps a clear record of what was in the original agreement and what was added or changed — useful for invoicing and for protecting yourself in disputes.

---

## Quote History

Every quote you've ever created stays in BuilderDash. Useful for:
- Re-quoting similar jobs quickly
- Reviewing what you quoted previously for a client
- Understanding your win/loss rate over time
