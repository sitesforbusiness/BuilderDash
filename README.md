# BuilderDash — Construction Project Management Software

**BuilderDash** is job management software built specifically for builders, tradespeople, and fit-out contractors.

> Manage quotes, jobs, invoices, scheduling, site photos, and client communication — all from one system.

🌐 **[builderdash.app](https://builderdash.app)**

---

## Who It's For

BuilderDash is built for real construction businesses that need a practical, no-nonsense system to run their day-to-day operations:

- **Builders** — residential and commercial
- **Fit-out contractors** — office fit-outs, retail, hospitality
- **Trades** — electricians, plumbers, carpenters, painters, and more
- **Small to mid-size construction companies**

If you're currently running your business on spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and paper invoices — BuilderDash is built for you.

---

## What BuilderDash Does

### 📋 Quotes & Estimates
Create professional quotes quickly. Send them to clients, track acceptance, and convert to jobs in one click.

### 🏗️ Job Management
Track every job from start to finish. Know what's happening on each site, who's assigned, and what's outstanding.

### 🧾 Invoices
Generate invoices directly from jobs. No double-entry. Get paid faster with professional invoice documents.

### 📅 Scheduling
Schedule your teams and tasks. See who's where and plan your week without juggling spreadsheets.

### 📸 Site Photos & Documents
Upload photos and documents directly to jobs. Keep everything in one place — no more hunting through WhatsApp or email.

### 💬 Client Communication
Keep clients updated without the back-and-forth. Log communication, send updates, and manage expectations.

## Why BuilderDash

Most construction software is either:
- Built for large enterprises with huge teams and IT departments
- So complicated it takes months to learn
- Priced out of reach for small to mid-size operators

**BuilderDash is different.** It's designed for the builder who's also the owner — someone who needs to quote on Monday, manage three sites on Tuesday, and invoice on Friday. Fast to set up, simple to use, and built around how construction businesses actually operate.
